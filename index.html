Luke Richards

Hi! I'm a current Ph.D student studying machine learning- specifically the intersection of bias, security, and robustness. I'm advised by Cynthia Matuszek and Edward Raff. I am a member of the Interactive Robotics & Language Lab at UMBC. I'm currently a Research Scientist at Booz Allen Hamilton focusing on the ML security, ML applied to security, and bias in ML with the context application to government. I'm also a deep advocate of inclusion within AI as a LGBTQ+ first-generation research scientist from a working class family.
